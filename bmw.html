BMW i8 Coupé

The future is taking shape – in the form of the new BMW i8 Coupé. Full of verve, fascinating and ready to newly define mobility. For unconditional driving pleasure, as far as the road goes. A mere glimpse of this iconic design elevates adrenaline levels. The tachometer shoots up just as fast: the innovative plug-in hybrid engine generates 374 hp and 570 Nm. Plus, it accelerates the new BMW i8 Coupé in just 4.4 seconds from 0 to 100 km. The fastest route to a new era.

Key Features

42 g/km

Drive ahead and leave practically nothing behind - thanks to the intelligent energy management of the BMW i8 Coupé. It coordinates the interaction between the electric motor and the battery with the combustion engine – always geared towards maximum performance with minimum consumption. This creates a powerful plug-in hybrid system with enormous efficiency, as evidenced by the combined consumption of only 1.8 l/100 km and CO2 emissions of up to 42 g/km.

A NEW FORM OF LIGHTWEIGHT

Take the lead. It’s easy with the new BMW i8 Coupé. One of the reasons is its exceptional lightweight concept: the LifeDrive Architecture. It consists of the Drive module – an aluminium chassis and the Life module – an ultralight passenger compartment made of high-strength carbon-fibre. Both components are weight-optimised down to the last gram in order to take thought-out sustainability with perfected sportiness efficiently to the road.

THE FREEDOM TO REACH EVERYTHING

Charging or refuelling – both are easy to handle in the new BMW i8 Coupé with the comprehensive product and service package 360° ELECTRIC. At home, you can easily charge your BMW i8 with the BMW i Wallbox or the charging cable. On the road, you’ll find an ever-growing number of public charging stations that you can easily use with the ChargeNow card. Out where the next charging station is directly in your BMW i8 Coupé: for example, via the fully-networked BMW ConnectedDrive services for navigation.
